How does extra vvirgin oil oil (EVOO) compare to regular olive oil?

EVOO is made from pure, cold-pressed olived and is certified as free of any defects in flavor or ordor, when tested by trained panel of experts. Regular olive oil is made from a blend of cold-pressed and processed oils.
